Unified inbox (multiple accounts)

Train one mailbox at a time. Your agent merges rules into one brief. You keep reading mail in Gmail, Apple Mail, or Outlook — we do not host your email.

When to use this

Turn this on when you have more than one mailbox (work + personal, etc.) and want the same agent to sort all of them with separate rules per account.

Leave it off if you only use one inbox — nothing changes for you.

What changes for you

BeforeAgent has no memory of how you sort mail
Training~15–30 min per account — swipe or learn from folders
AfterOne brief with a section per inbox; agent recommends actions in chat
Your mail appsUnchanged — same Gmail / Apple Mail / Outlook

How a session works

  1. Your agent registers each mailbox (name, provider) in settings — in chat, not in this form.
  2. Agent loads mail for one account and starts the swipe UI.
  3. Header shows which account you are training.
  4. Repeat for each mailbox.
  5. Agent reads the combined brief with you and helps triage new mail going forward.

What we do not do

  • Host or store your mail on our servers
  • Replace your mail app
  • Auto-delete or hide mail without your approval
  • Ask for your life story — if your agent already knows your calendar or projects, it uses that on its own
