Autonomy tiers

How much your agent can do without asking each time. Email Swipe never auto-deletes mail at any tier.

Recommend (default)

Your agent suggests labels, folders, and actions in chat. You approve before anything changes in your mailbox.

Approve batch

Your agent can propose a batch of safe actions (e.g. label a group of promos) and wait for one approval. Still no delete or skip-inbox without you.

Auto safe

Your agent may apply only the narrowest rules you have explicitly trained — still bounded by forbidden actions in Advanced settings → Rules. Use when you trust the brief and want fewer interruptions.

Change tier in Advanced settings → Agent. Higher tiers require confirmation in the UI.
